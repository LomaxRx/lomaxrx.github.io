About Me

>

I’m partly a designer, partly a creative, mostly a software engineer, and entirely an idealist.

I’ve been working as a technologist at nonprofits in DC for 7 years, which is to say, I’ve been learning the art of doing a lot with a little for 7 years. Little money, little time, small teams. So I’ve learned to be resourceful, empathetic, and efficient. I'm collecting my thoughts about my work so far here.

I grew up in Virginia, spent 4 years in Providence, RI, graduating from Brown in 2010, and I guess DC is my de facto home, although I still consider myself a nomad.
